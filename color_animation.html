Assignment:
Colors and Animations

About the project

This assignment focuses on the compatibility of colors and the importance of checking said compatibility before implementation on a website. Using Adobe's color contrast checker I have found colors that work in either light or dark mode, ensuring that the text is readable and the CTA links and buttons still stand out in good contrast.

Selected details

Light and Dark theme

Here is an overview of the colors used in light and dark mode. Even though the colors are different, the palette I have used is still complementary to each other. The inspiration for the full color palette is the Nord Theme color palette.

Light theme color contrast

The light theme color palette consists of a very light grey as background color (#ECEFF4), a dark grey/blue for headers and highlights (#4C566A), a light blue for graphics (#5E81AC), and two accent colors for buttons and links, one yellow (#ECD0A3) and red (#9B5057). Text color is either the dark blue/grey on the light background, or the light grey on the darker highlight backgrounds.

Dark theme color contrast

The dark theme color palette uses the darker highlight color from the light theme as the general background color. The red accent color is not used in this theme, only the yellow for buttons and links. For header and highlights an even darker blue is used (#2E3440), and the graphics are a light green (#8FBCBB).
